Studio Opening
January 2022

Alexandra is a certified pilates teacher from Body Control Pilates in London-UK. Teaching to any 'body' and everybody. 

As a mum of 3, she used the method to heal and recover in a safe and efficient way. Following a holistic approach to life, has given her the ability to connect with her private clients on a deeper level. 

Alexandra has been teaching in virtual classes through zoom during the pandemic and decided to open her own private studio in late 2021.
